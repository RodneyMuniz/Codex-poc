# Archer UI Notes

## UI Concepts

- **Clear Unit Identity**: Archer should be distinctly recognizable through UI elements that reflect agility, ranged combat, and precision.
- **Minimalist HUD**: Display essential stats like range, ammo/arrows remaining, and special abilities with succinct icons to avoid clutter.
- **Contextual Action Buttons**: Actions such as “Shoot,” “Move,” or “Special Attack” appear dynamically according to Archer’s current context and cooldowns.
- **Range Indicators**: On the tactical map, show an overlay or subtle highlighting indicating the maximum attack range and possible target tiles.
- **Target Highlighting**: When selecting the Archer or targeting an enemy, highlight valid targets with a distinct color or outline for fast readability.

## Visual Direction

- **Color Palette**: Use earthy, natural tones such as greens and browns complemented by sharp accent colors like deep oranges or amber to reflect the Archer’s connection with nature and focus.
- **Iconography**: Employ arrow and bow motifs for buttons and ability icons; simple shapes maintain clarity at small sizes.
- **Animation and Effects**: Subtle animations on the Archer portrait or UI frame when selecting or performing actions (e.g., a quick bow-draw motion or arrow nock).
- **Spellcasting Visuals**: When casting ranged or special abilities, show a simple glowing effect on the bow or aimed arrow icon, combined with faint projectile trails on the map.
- **Typography**: Use clean, legible fonts with slight angular styling to evoke precision and sharpness.

## Player Feedback

- **Action Confirmation**: Provide immediate visual and audio feedback when an Archer action is executed—e.g., brief arrow trail animation and “release” sound.
- **Cooldown and Availability**: Clearly indicate ability cooldown timers using progress bars or grayscale overlay on icons.
- **Hit/Miss Indicators**: Show floating textual or icon feedback near enemy units indicating hit damage or miss to reinforce battle outcomes.
- **Status Effects**: Communicate buffs or debuffs on the Archer via small, unobtrusive badge icons near the health bar or unit frame.
- **Focus and Selection**: When the player hovers over or selects the Archer, emphasize with subtle glow or outline animations to reinforce engagement.
